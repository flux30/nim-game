The Nim Game

Welcome to Nim, a strategic game of mathematical prowess where the last move determines the winner.

How to Play:

  • You and the AI take turns removing balls from a heap.
  • On each turn, you must remove 1, 2, or 3 balls.
  • The player forced to take the last ball loses.
  • Choose your difficulty level and who goes first.
  • Can you outsmart the AI? (Spoiler: It's quite difficult!)

The game starts with a pile of 15 balls. Strategy is key - the player who understands the mathematical pattern behind Nim will have the advantage.
